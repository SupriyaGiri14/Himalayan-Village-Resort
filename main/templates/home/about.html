About Us

It is picturesquely set amongst the scenic beauty of the famous hill station, kausani. kausani is famous for its scenic splendour and its spectacular 300 km-wide panoramic view of Himalayan peaks like Trisul, Nanda Devi and Panchchuli. Surrounded by undulating hills and imbued with simple elegance, the property is spread across 7 luxurious suites, nestled in pairs across five structures with private overlapping terraces offering views to the Great Himalayan range.

All the cottages are tea garden-facing and through the windows, guests can behold breathtaking sights of the Himalayas. The resort boasts of a stylish restaurant called Bhumika that serves a variety of mouth-watering Indian, Chinese, Mughlai, South Indian, and Kumaouni dishes. Within the premises, there is also a coffee shop where guests can savour special Kausani garden tea, herbal tea, coffee, juices and fast foods. Besides offering comfortable accommodation, Himalayan Village Resort offers ample indoor and outdoor activities like bonfire with music, nature trekking, cultural programs, yoga and meditations, bird watching and more.
